How the Tournament Works

3-round knockout for adults · 2-round format for kids

🎲 Ludo boards have 4 players each. Each game is played to completion. Rankings within each board determine who advances. Top 2 advance in Round 1 — only the winner advances from Round 2 onwards.
🧑 Adults Path
Round 1 → Round 2 → Final
  • 1Round 1 — 8 Boards (31 players)
  • 2Round 2 — 4 Boards of 4
  • 3🏆 Final — 4 players, 1 board
✅ Top 2 per board advance in R1
✅ Only winner advances R2 onwards
How elimination works
  • R13rd & 4th place → Eliminated
  • R22nd, 3rd, 4th → Eliminated
  • F2nd, 3rd, 4th → Runners-up
